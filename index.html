About

Dr. Madan Kumar Khadka (PhD, in Landscape Ecology from the Vegetation Structure and Process Research Group, School of Ecology and Environmental Sciences, Yunnan University, Kunming, China, 2025) is an Senior Scientific Officer at Department of Plant Resources. He has held Scientific Officer positions at different Plant Research Centers and researcher positions at Yunnan University and Tribhuvan University. His work blends vegetation ecology, ecosystem modeling, and geospatial analysis of environmental change in the Himalayas.

Selected roles: Researcher | Landscape Ecologist | GIS & Remote Sensing Specialist

Research interests

  • Vegetation ecology and biodiversity dynamics
  • GIS and remote sensing applications in ecology
  • Climate change modeling and adaptation
  • Medicinal and aromatic plant distribution
  • Vulnerability assessment and Species distribution modelling of MAPs/NTFPs
